About

Julie M Berryhill has lived in Charlotte since 1996 and started in Real Estate in 1998 which has allowed her to witness and be a part of the exponential growth of the City . Julie relocated from the Midwest and has helped numerous clients with relocating and acclimating to Charlotte. She has worked in New Construction and General Brokerage Real Estate, helping buyers, sellers and investors. Julie lives one mile from Uptown in her seventh home in Charlotte. She has worked all over Mecklenburg County as well as surrounding areas. She likes to remodel homes on the side and has a lot of contacts to help you improve or transform your new home into the home of your dreams. She is very knowledgeable when it comes to financing options so please feel free to ask her questions.
